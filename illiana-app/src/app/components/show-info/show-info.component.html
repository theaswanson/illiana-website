Rules for the use of motorized vehicles during the annual show

  1. Only people with a valid driver's license can operate any motorized vehicle on the showgrounds. No exceptions.
  2. Anyone operating a motorized vehicle must have proof of insurance or a signed liability waiver (available on the grounds).
  3. Golf carts or side-by-sides are not allowed on the grounds during show hours without special permission from the board of directors.
  4. Handicap individuals may use a golf cart with proof of insurance or a liability waiver, and an approved sticker displayed on the vehicle (stickers available at the gate).
  5. An approved IAPA sticker indicates that the golf cart is approved for use on IAPA grounds.
  6. Golf carts can only be used by Board of Directors or persons with approval from the president.
  7. Anyone not willing to abide by the above rules may be asked to leave the showgrounds.

IAPA Ground Rules

  • No alcohol
  • No firearms
  • No knives visible
  • Dogs must be on a leash at all times. No dogs in hall near food area
  • No camping inside the showgrounds
  • Quiet time: 11pm-8am
