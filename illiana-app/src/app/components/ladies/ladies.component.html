Ladies Activities

During our annual show each July, the Illiana Quilters are on the grounds showing and selling their crafts! Throughout the year, the Illiana Quilters meet on the fourth Monday of each month at the Assembly Hall at 9:00 AM (except in July and December).

For more information, contact Ann Bodine at abodine1@wildblue.net or send a message to the Illiana Quilters Facebook page.
