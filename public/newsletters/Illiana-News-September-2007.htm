ILLIANA NEWS

SEPTEMBER 2007                                      ISSUE 9

 

MEETING - DIRECTORS ONLY:

There will not be a  September meeting of the Illiana Antique Power Assoc.  Instead, the directors will meet on September 17, 2007 at 6:30 P.M. at the club house.  (No carry-in dinner).  The next regular club meeting will be in October.

 

SYMPATHY:

Our deepest sympathy goes to club member Wayburn Norfleet.  His wife passed away on August 17, 2007.

 

REQUEST:

Terry Bodine is still in need of some 5-gal plastic buckets to cover the outlets around the show grounds.  If you have one to spare, please bring it on 9-30-07.

 

     FUN DAY!

Our annual “Fun Day” is scheduled to be held on September 30, 2007 at the show grounds.

We will have a carry-in dinner at 2:00 P.M.

We will also be pressing apples from the Coffing Bros. Orchard but, if you have some apples (or pears) you’d like to press, please bring them along.  Also, don’t forget to bring your own plastic jugs so you can take some cider home!

Come as early as you like.....the railroad group should have the trains “fired up!”  Don Bodine will have his steam engine fired up as well.  If  you have a hobby you’d like to share, bring it along to show us.  Ask a friend, bring him along!
