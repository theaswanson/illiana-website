Our annual “Fun Day” is scheduled to be held on September 21, 2008!!

ROCKY FORGE BLACKSMITHS NEWS:

After a three week recovery from the Illiana show, the blacksmith group spent two very long days demonstrating at the Indiana State Fair.  We had a good turnout with six on the first day and nine during the second day.  If you have never been to the Pioneer Village at the State Fair, you are missing out on a good one.  Our Illiana member Kathy Olin, has worked there for many years as well as other notable craft people.  The old farm machinery and home displays are really worth seeing.  Our next meeting will be September 13th,  just down the road from the Illiana show grounds, at Greg Searcy’s located in Rainsville.  We are looking forward to that meeting on the cliffs of Pine Creek.  Meeting time is 9:00 A.M. and a pitch in lunch will be at about noon.  Greg is cooking his famous pork tenderloin.  In the event of rain, we will move the meeting to our new building.           Submitted by: Ted Stout

 

PINE CREEK AND WESTERN R.R. NEWS:

Recent track repairs and changes have made railroading better than ever on the old  PC&W!  With that in mind, we’re hoping to get some engines out for Fun Day.  Members are urged to bring whatever equipment they can and enjoy the day.  Seems new railroader, Don Evans, has bought himself some great engines and rolling stock and will soon be burning up the rails he worked so hard to improve this summer.  Welcome aboard!!  Don, glad to see new interest in the trains.  I hope to organize another “Steam up” before the weather goes bad, but we’ll see.  “Keep Em’ Rollin!       Submitted by: Finny Filchak
