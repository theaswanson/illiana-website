THANK YOU:

     I’d like to thank everyone who helped on our scheduled workday, November 5th, to get ready for “Christmas in the Village”.  We got quite a lot accomplished!  The leaves were raked and burned and the grounds were mowed.  The clubhouse was cleaned, the train board was set up, lights were hung around the grounds and buildings and the luminaries are ready to set out. (Thanks for saving those milk jugs!)  We had a delicious meal with some great desserts served for lunch.

     On Saturday, December 10th we’ll be serving Chili at 7:00 P.M. at the close of the show.  Bring a dish to pass (if you want to – it’s not mandatory) and join us for some Holiday Fun!  Don’t forget to bring your cameras – SANTA WILL BE THERE!

Tom Swanson & Committee Members
