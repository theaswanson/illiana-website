Thank-you Tom & Carolyn Swanson......

Once again club members enjoyed a “trip to Fantasy land” at the October meeting held at Tom and Carolyn Swanson’s lovely country home.  After a delicious carry-in dinner and a short meeting, Tom opened the door to his wonderful display of miniature builngs he constructed and Carolyn decorated.  There were row after row of  replicas of area businesses and houses, nativity scenes, collectable items, vintage photos and an antique map of Indiana hanging on the wall.  Even though many of us had enjoyed this wonder-land before (this was our third year at the Swanson’s) there was something new to discover.  Thank you Tom and Carolyn for sharing your hobby and talents with us.  (Do we dare to hope we’ll be invited back again next year!)
